References

This page gathers readings, resources, and practical materials relevant to research methods, fieldwork, and scholarly support in difficult research environments.

About this Section

The References section is intended as a shared resource for scholars seeking practical and intellectual support in conducting research in conflict-affected, hostile, or politically sensitive environments. It will continue to expand over time.

Suggested Areas

  • Ethnography and fieldwork in hostile or difficult settings
  • Research ethics, access, and safety
  • Emotion, care, and positionality in fieldwork
  • Methods for researching digital illicit economies
  • Resources for early-career researchers
